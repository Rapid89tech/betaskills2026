## Learning Objectives

Students will be able to:
1. Develop compelling podcast concepts with defined niche and audience
2. Master audio recording and editing software (Audacity, Adobe Audition)
3. Implement professional microphone and recording techniques
4. Create and execute content plans with episode scripting and guest management
5. Publish podcasts on major platforms (Spotify, Apple Podcasts) with optimized metadata
6. Apply marketing strategies for social media promotion and audience engagement
7. Analyze podcast performance metrics to refine content and increase reach
