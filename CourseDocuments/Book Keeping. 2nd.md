1. ### **Definition of Bookkeeping.**

Bookkeeping serves as the foundational process of systematically recording, organising, and maintaining financial transactions in a business or personal context, capturing daily income, expenses, assets, and liabilities to provide an accurate ongoing picture of financial health that supports informed decisions and compliance.
